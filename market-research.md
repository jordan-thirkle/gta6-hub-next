# GTA 6 Hub — Market Research
## GTA 5 App Landscape
- GTAForums, GTA5-Mods.com, GTA Base dominate conversation; mostly forums/wiki/map wrappers
- Companion apps are mostly forum wrappers or niche map tools
- Revenue is modest unless you own the distribution or ad inventory at scale
- No one has succeeded with a modern PWA + mobile app + SEO-first strategy

## Revenue Reality
- Game companion apps rarely hit blockbuster ARPU
- Best play: affiliate + display ads + newsletter sponsorship
- Scale matters more than CPM: volume + SEO is the moat
- Modest RPM compounds fast if you catch the search wave

## Viral Windows
- Search volume explodes 3–6 months before launch
- Content indexed now captures long-tail terms before launch
- Trailer react posts and edition guides are highest intent
- Creator traffic around launch is massive if share cards are ready

## Monetization Plan
- Affiliate: Amazon/Epic/Humble guide links
- Display: TinyAdz/Carbon-style placements with CLS 0 rules
- Sponsored: edition/guide sponsorships
- Newsletter: weekly curated digest
- Optional: ad-free PWA mode
