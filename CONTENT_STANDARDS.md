# GTA 6 Hub — Content Standards
## Fact Classification Rules
- `confirmed`: official Rockstar source, first-party reveal, or hard publisher confirmation.
- `credible`: industry-standard leak/reporting with repeatable sourcing or analyst consensus.
- `speculative`: estimation, community mapping, or unverified claim.

## Required Metadata
Every published fact must include:
- `sourceType`
- `sourceName`
- `sourceUrl` when available

## Prohibited Content
- fabricated specs, fake screenshots, or persona-based rumors
- claims presented as fact without a labeled source level
- quantified revenue/sales figures without verified reporting links

## Review Gate
- No article ships without a source audit
- Unverified items must be removed or downgraded to `speculative`
- User-facing claim count must remain lean and accurate
